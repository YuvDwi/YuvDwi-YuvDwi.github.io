About Me

Hi again! You've come to the about me section and just as expected, I'll start rambling about my passions!

The idea of entrepreneurship has never failed to fascinate me. Solve a problem and learn real-world skills in the process, why in the world not? This passion led me to co-founding Hush. At Hush, my team and I work to apply deep-learning algorithms to create universally-compatible noise cancelling software. The intersection between technology and business is constantly evolving, and being at the forefront of this point is profusely exciting!

Amidst all this, I've learnt C++, Python, HTML, CSS, JS and Java. This led me to develop mobile apps, robotic software, games, websites, and my favorite, video games!.
